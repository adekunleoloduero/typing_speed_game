TYPIX

Typix is a simple typing game that presents the player with a random quote and measures the time taken by the player to type it out accurately.

How to Play

  1. Click "Play" to start the game.
  2. In the field labeled "Your Response" type out the quote that is displayed.
  3. Check the time taken to finish typing the quote correctly.
  4. Click "Play" to start again.
